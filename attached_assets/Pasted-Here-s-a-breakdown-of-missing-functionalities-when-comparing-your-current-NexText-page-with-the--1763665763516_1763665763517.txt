***

**Features visible in the attached Messenger UI design but missing or not implemented in NexText:**

1. **Dark Theme Support**

2. **Chat Media Previews**

3. **Reactions and Emoji**

4. **Voice/Video Calling**
    - Call screens (audio/video call UI) and in-call actions (mute, video toggle, end call).
    - Missing: Voice and video calling feature.

5. **Message Status Indicators**
    - Messenger displays sent, delivered, read ticks or labels.
    - Missing: Read receipts, delivery status, failed status.

6. **Stories/Status**
    - Some screens show profile stories/status visible to contacts.
    - Missing: Ability to upload/view ephemeral status/stories.

7. **Group Chats**
    - Messenger supports group chat creation and group member management.
    - Missing: Group chat, add/remove members, group info.

8. **Profile and Settings**
    - Messenger UI shows profile, account, settings screens.
    - Missing: Profile management, change avatar, update status, edit account info, privacy settings.

9. **Search & Filters**
    - Messenger supports advanced search – by contact/message/attachments.
    - Missing: Search messages, filter by date/media.

10. **Notification & Invite**
    - Screens for invitations, notifications, requests.
    - Missing: Manage friend requests/invites, notification screen.

11. **Chat Customization**
    - Messenger allows changing chat themes, colors, emojis per chat.
    - Missing: Per-chat customization (theme, emoji, nickname).

12. **Attachment Options**
    - Messenger can send files, stickers, GIFs.
    - Missing: File attachment, stickers/GIF support.

13. **Archived/Deleted Chats**
    - Messenger offers archiving/deleting/hiding chats.
    - Missing: Archive, delete, restore chat.

14. **Group Features:**
    - Messenger shows group participants, group photo, edit group name/avatar.
    - Missing: Group settings UI.

15. **Contact/Directory**
    - Messenger allows contact search, add/remove/block.
    - Missing: Manage contacts, block/unblock users.
